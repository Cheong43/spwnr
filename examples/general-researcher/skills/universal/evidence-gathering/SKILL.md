# Evidence Gathering

Use this skill to produce a compact research brief.

## Focus

- what is true in the codebase or supplied context
- what constraints the user has stated
- what options are realistically available
- what risks or unknowns remain

## Output Standard

Always organize the result into:

- findings
- assumptions
- recommendation
- blockers
